<h1>Road Accident Analysis</h1>
A data Analysis project on Road accidents in UK, 2023 - 2024 Using Microsoft Excel

The data has been sourced from Here
<h3>Process :</h3>
First i explored 3.07 Million Record on Road accidents for 2023 and 2024.
Second i have done some data cleaning on the data
Third i have done some analysis by asking/answering some questions
then Built the dasboard

<h3>Analysis :</h3>
<li>Analysing road accidents based upon the Road Type</li>
<li>Analysis of accidents according to the Type of vehicel</li>
<li>Analysis of accidents as per the Location / Area</li>
<li>Analysis of accidents based upon the TIME “Day or light”</li>
<li>Analysis the distribution of total Casualties by road surface</li>
<li>Analysis monthly trend that shwoing the comparison of Casualties for current year 2023 and previous year 2024</li>
<li>Understanding the CAUSE AND REASONS behind the accidents, injuries and deaths of people.</li>
